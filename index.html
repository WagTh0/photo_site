About:

I am an amateur, yet serious, landscape photographer based in Oregon (for now). I am always looking for hidden and beautiful locations to photograph and new compositions to try. I like taking pictures that have many layers and make full use of available space. I also like trying to convey how small we are compared to the grand landscape. My photography so far covers only Washington, Oregon and California, but in the coming years, I want to expand that out to the Rocky Mountains and the Great Basin Ranges.

My gear is modest. Only a 24 Megapixel Sony a6000 APS-C camera with a set of 4 lenses, some polarizers, GND filters, an electronic cable shutter release and a hefty tripod have taken all these photos. Adobe Photoshop is used for correcting color casts, sharpening and noise reduction. Each picture is a true representation of what I saw with my own eyes as I triggered the shutter.

Although my pictures cover many landscapes, the following areas are covered more extensively:

  • Mount Hood (OR)
  • Wallowa Mountains (OR)
  • Mount Rainier (WA)

Other less extensively covered areas include:

  • Oregon Coast (OR)
  • Columbia River Gorge (OR,WA)
  • Three Sisters (OR)
  • Mount Adams (WA)
  • Goat Rocks (WA)
  • Elkhorn Mountains (OR)
  • Strawberry Mountains (OR)
  • Sierra Nevada (CA)
  • Death Valley (CA)...

...among many other areas that are covered in my collection by a single shot.
